Cmus

Following the commandline trend, I'm also a big fan of the cmus project. Cmus is a commandline music manager. It loosely follows vim keybindings and is rock solid. There is also plugin support but I haven't actually had anything that I wanted that wasn't built in already. Here is the link to the github.io page.
